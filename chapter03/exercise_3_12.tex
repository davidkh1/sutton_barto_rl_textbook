\section*{Problem}

Give an equation for $v_\pi$ in terms of $q_\pi$ and $\pi$.

\section*{Solution}

The state-value function is the expected return starting from state $s$
and following policy $\pi$. Since $\pi$ selects actions stochastically,
we average $q_\pi$ over all actions weighted by the policy:

\[
    \boxed{v_\pi(s) = \sum_{a} \pi(a \mid s) \; q_\pi(s, a)}
\]

This follows directly from the definitions:
\begin{align*}
    v_\pi(s) &\doteq \mathbb{E}_\pi[G_t \mid S_t = s] \\
             &= \sum_{a} \pi(a \mid s) \; \mathbb{E}_\pi[G_t \mid S_t = s, A_t = a] \\
             &= \sum_{a} \pi(a \mid s) \; q_\pi(s, a)
\end{align*}

where the second step uses the law of total expectation, conditioning on
the action taken.
